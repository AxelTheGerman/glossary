Net Income The Profit left for the owners, after all costs, expenses, interest and taxes have been paid. Also called Net Profit. [ros] Ratio: [ni] as a percentage of [sa] Net Income (or Net Profit) is the Profit left for the owners, after all costs, expenses, interest and taxes have been paid. Example: • Sales of 200 less COS (80) less Operating expense (70) less interest expense (20) and income tax (10) leaves Net Income of 20. • Net Income of 20 divided by Sales of 200 equals Return on Sales of 10%.
