Profit & Cash - de

Die deutsche Fassung wird zur Zeit noch uebersetzt... Understanding the Cash Flow of a business is as essential as understanding Profit. [prof] is like food - you need it for the business to grow and to be healthy and strong; but you don’t have to eat all the time. But cash is like air – you need it constantly. If you run out, and cannot immediately get more, you die. Profit happens on the [is]. Cash happens on the [bs]. The two have to be managed separately.
