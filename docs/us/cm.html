Contribution Margin - us

CONTRIBUTION Calculation: [sa] less the [cos]. CONTRIBUTION MARGIN Ratio: [cont] as a percentage of [sa] Contribution is what’s left from the sale to contribute towards paying for the overhead costs and expenses of the business. This is a real [wdym] term with people sometimes using the two terms interchangeably. We use ‘margin’ to denote a percentage (of Sales). Example: - Sales of 200 less COS of 80 equals Contribution of 120 - Contribution of 120 divided by Sales of 200 equals Contribution Margin of 60%.
