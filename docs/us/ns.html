Net Sales - us

Calculation: [gs] at list price less any discounts, sales returns and allowances, but before subtracting the [cos] and [oe]. Revenues from the sale of goods and services which are part of the company’s regular operations. Net Sales is the number used in Financial Statements if the line item just says ’Sales.’ [wdym] The term ‘Sales’ generally refers to Net Sales. The term ‘Total Sales’ could refer to [gs] or Net Sales.
